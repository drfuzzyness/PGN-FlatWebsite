What's New with PGN.

We host several events and tournaments each semester and support several events with the NYU PLAY club. We ask that you keep the server usage to just you and your friends (even outside of NYU), but not too much farther than that. If you have an idea for an event for us to host, let us know!

PGN UPDATE

We'd like to thank everyone once again for stopping by to our Club Fest booth at Poly a couple weeks ago!  We had a great time and hope everyone will enjoy what we have in store for the semester.

For those of you who use FaceBook as a way to keep in touch with your groups and clubs, we have unveiled our new Facebook group that now accepts any student with a valid @nyu.edu email!  Come join your fellow gamers HERE!

We are also releasing our new applications to become a featured streamer and/or contributor to the PGN!  This means that your stream or articles/videos/consumable gaming media will be promoted by the PGN via our various pages and social media outlets!  This also means you have a chance to become a featured streamer/contributor of the week/month as we move forward with generating content for the community!  Here are the applications for STREAMING and CONTENT GENERATION.

As for events planned for the upcoming semester, here's a quick summary of what is currently in the works:

  1. We will be hosting a League of Legends weekly tourney, starting in October, featuring weekly games, casting, prizing support, and livestreaming that will all culminate in a Finals LAN party with free food and giveaways!  Registration opens this weekend, so find friends or sign up as a free agent and prepare to take to the Rift!
  2. Our Pokemon event may now be held in conjunction with NYU PLAY which means more people, more fun, more prizes and more food!  Slated for late fall to coincide with the new games coming out, get hyped for Omega Red and Alpha Sapphire as well as finding out who might just become NYU's Pokemon Master!
  3. Keeping in the Nintendo theme, upcoming big things for Super Smash Brothers! Keep up to date with our newsletters to find out the final details once we get formal approval and finish planning! Get hyped and ready to pop off and watch some great Smash play!
  4. Our first offline event will be held during the middle of October, currently pending an official date.  Please come out and show your support so that we can continue hosting offline, free-food events!  We will be taking requests for games and will try to bring as many as possible moving forward!
  5. We will also be hosting various online events for games featured on streams and on our servers!  If you didn't already know, you can get all the information for our private servers via our website! Just log-in and you'll have access to the tab!  Furthermore, you can totally bring friends who don't go to NYU to join in and play! (at least for now!)  Just don't post the info all over the internet ^_~

  1. League of Legends
  2. Hearthstone
  3. DotA2
  4. Melee
  5. TF2

We also would love it for people to turn out and cheer on our school!  More details about each event to come!
