What We're All About.

We're gamers and eSports fans at NYU based in Brooklyn. Together, we host touraments, gaming groups, online streams, servers, and eSports viewing parties for NYU students.

Join In Contact Us

The mission of Poly Gaming Network is to create an environment for Poly students to appreciate the technical and recreational aspects of video games.

Poly Gaming Network was founded in October 2013. This organization is a part of NYU-Poly and strives towards bringing the joys and wonders of video games to the Poly community .

We also play games.

We're a club at NYU's School of Engineering

We're funded by the NYU SOE Student Council and lead by these fine students. We're predominantly undergraduates, but all you graduates out there are totally welcome.
